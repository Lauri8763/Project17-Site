About us

Yoko's Kitchen - school, which introducing it's pupils to Japanese cookery and teaching them the mastery of cooking Japanese food and dishes. Yoko's Kitchen was established in 1982 by Yoko Arasaka, famous cooker who decided to share his knowledge and skills with other people. Even after his death in 2012 his school is still functioning and continues to share it's wisdom with all people, who interested in Japanese cuisines.

Our school offering various activities, starting with history of Japanese cookery till the master classes with professional cookers, cooking classes and catering services. On our website you can also find a lot of our unique receipts for a countless Japanese dishes, soups, sauses, meat, pancakes, vegetarian food and et cetera.
As the saying goes - "We have everything and even more!".
Everyone, who is interested in Japanese meals can find something for themselves.
